Paula Deen

Butter Queen!

Paula Ann Hiers Deen is an American TV personality and cooking show host, best known for her use of butter. Deen resides in Savannah, Georgia, where she owns and operates The Lady & Sons restaurant and Paula Deen's Creek House with her sons, Jamie and Bobby Deen. She has published fifteen cookbooks
